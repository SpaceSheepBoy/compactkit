How do I batch compress photos?
  1. Choose multiple JPG, PNG, or WebP photos.
  2. Pick output format, quality, and max size.
  3. Click Compress photos. The compressed images download as one ZIP.
Are photos uploaded?

No. The photos are processed in your browser and zipped locally.

Which output should I pick?

Pick JPEG for general sharing, WebP for smaller web images, and PNG when you need lossless output.
