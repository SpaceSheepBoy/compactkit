How do I speed up audio?
  1. Choose an audio file.
  2. Pick 1.25x, 1.5x, or 2x speed.
  3. Click Change speed. The faster M4A file downloads when ready.
Can I slow audio down?

Yes. Choose 0.5x or 0.75x to make the audio slower.

Are files uploaded?

No. The audio speed change runs locally in your browser.
