Quick checks

  • If the camera view does not appear, check Camera permission in iOS Settings.
  • If saving fails, check Photos permission and available device storage.
  • If capture is blocked after several shots, you may have reached the free-shot limit. Lifetime Unlock removes the limit.
  • If Lifetime Unlock does not activate after purchase, open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Premium filters stay locked until you purchase Lifetime Unlock or use all free shots and open the paywall.

Lifetime Unlock

Lifetime Unlock is a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase. It unlocks unlimited photo capture and all premium retro filters. Payment and purchase history are handled by Apple.
