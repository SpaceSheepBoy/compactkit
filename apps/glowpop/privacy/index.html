SummaryGlowPop is designed to work on your device. We do not intentionally collect your photos, videos, camera feed, microphone recordings, advertising identifiers, precise location, or contacts.

Effective date: June 8, 2026

What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to GlowPop, including screen lighting, selfie photo and video capture, Live Photo capture, retro filtered saves, support, and purchase-related workflows.

Camera, microphone, and Photos

  • Camera access is used to show the front-camera preview and capture selfies, videos, and Live Photos.
  • Microphone access is used only when recording selfie videos with sound.
  • Photos access is used to save photos, videos, Live Photos, and filtered exports when you choose to save them.

You can review or revoke these permissions in iOS Settings.

Data we do not intentionally collect

  • Your camera preview, captured photos, videos, Live Photos, or filtered exports.
  • Your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or advertising identifiers.
  • Account credentials, because GlowPop does not require a Just Compress account.

Local app data

GlowPop may store app preferences on your device, such as selected light mode, color, brightness, speed, shape, capture settings, and Pro entitlement state. Output files are saved to Photos only when you choose to save them.

Purchases

GlowPop Pro is available as a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase. Apple processes payment and purchase history. GlowPop uses StoreKit to show localized pricing, complete purchases, and restore purchases.

Diagnostics and App Store analytics

Apple may provide aggregated crash reports, performance information, and App Store analytics to developers. These reports depend on your Apple device settings and are used to improve reliability.

Third-party services

The app may use Apple frameworks and App Store services for camera capture, media saving, purchases, sharing, diagnostics, and distribution. GlowPop does not use advertising SDKs, App Tracking Transparency, or IDFA.

Support

If you contact support, we receive the email address and details you choose to send. Support questions can be sent to abel0911@icloud.com.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as GlowPop changes. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
