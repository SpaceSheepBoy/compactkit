GlowPop

Use your screen as a glow light for selfies, Live Photos, videos, color lighting, light shapes, dynamic modes, and retro filtered saves.

Need help?Email abel0911@icloud.com with the app name, iPhone or iPad model, iOS version, and what happened.

Quick checks

  • If the camera view does not appear, check Camera permission in iOS Settings.
  • If video recording has no sound, check Microphone permission in iOS Settings.
  • If saving fails, check Photos permission and available device storage.
  • If Pro features do not unlock after purchase, open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Keep the app open while saving retro filtered photos, especially for larger images.

Pro unlock

GlowPop Pro is a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase. It unlocks extra glow colors, custom color picking, light shapes, candle mode, RGB flow mode, and saving retro filtered photos. Payment and purchase history are handled by Apple.
