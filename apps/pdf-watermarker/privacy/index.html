Effective date: May 15, 2026

What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy applies to Just PDF Watermark, including the app's file processing, export, support, and purchase-related workflows.

Files and media processing

Just PDF Watermark works with PDF documents you select that you choose. Processing is designed to happen locally on your device. Your selected files are not intentionally uploaded to Just Compress servers.

Data we do not intentionally collect

  • The contents of your selected files, documents, photos, videos, or audio recordings.
  • Your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or advertising identifiers.
  • Account credentials, because the app does not require a Just Compress account.

Local app data

  • Watermark presets, recent settings, and app preferences may be stored on your device.
  • Output files are saved only when you choose to export or share them.

Permissions

  • Files access is limited to PDF documents you choose.

You can review or revoke app permissions in iOS Settings.

Purchases

The app is distributed through the App Store. Any purchase or download transaction is handled by Apple.

Diagnostics and App Store analytics

Apple may provide aggregated crash reports, performance information, and App Store analytics to developers. These reports depend on your Apple device settings and are used to improve reliability.

Third-party services

The app may use Apple frameworks and App Store services for file picking, media access, purchases, sharing, diagnostics, and distribution. We do not use advertising SDKs or sell personal data.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
