Help & FAQ

How to add a text watermark safely

Choose the right placement for drafts, confidential copies, names, and dates.

Quick tip: Choose PDF, type text, pick placement. Your file stays on your device.
How do I add a watermark to a PDF, step by step?
  1. Choose your PDF. Click Choose PDF above or drag a file in. It stays on your device.
  2. Type your watermark. Enter text such as CONFIDENTIAL, DRAFT, or your name.
  3. Pick a placement and opacity. Tiled diagonal covers the page, single diagonal is one large stamp in the centre, footer adds a line at the bottom.
  4. Click Add watermark. Every page is stamped, then the watermarked PDF downloads automatically.

Are my files uploaded?

No. Your PDF stays on your device, which is better for drafts, contracts, client files, and confidential documents.

Does it add a watermark of its own?

No. The only text on your PDF is the watermark you type. There's no logo, badge, or extra mark added to the output, and no sign-up.

How do I watermark a PDF on iPhone?
  • In Safari: open this page on your iPhone, choose your PDF from Files, and stamp it here.
  • With the app: install the free PDF Watermarker app to stamp documents offline and on the go.
Can I remove a watermark with this tool?

No. This tool only adds a watermark. It's designed to mark your own documents as draft or confidential, not to remove marks from files you didn't create.
