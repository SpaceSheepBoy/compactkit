Help & FAQ

How to make a video smaller safely

Use the right setting for short clips, messages, and email.

Quick tip: Choose video, pick quality, compress. Best for short clips.
How do I compress a video, step by step?
  1. Choose your video. Click Choose video above or drag a file in. MP4, MOV, M4V, and WebM work.
  2. Pick a quality level. Smallest compresses hardest, Balanced is the best default for sharing, High keeps more detail.
  3. Click Compress. Just Compress makes a smaller MP4 and downloads it automatically. This can take a little while.
Why compress in the browser instead of uploading?

Most online video compressors upload your file first. Just Compress keeps the video on your device, so it is better for personal clips. Because your device does the work, short clips work best.

How do I get the smallest video?
  • Use the Smallest quality level for messaging and email.
  • Trim the clip first — shorter video means a smaller file and faster compression.
  • For long videos or many files at once, an app is usually faster.
