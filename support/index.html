Quick checks

  • Keep the original file until you have opened and checked the output.
  • Try a shorter file or lower quality setting if the browser runs out of memory.
  • For iOS apps, confirm Files or Photos permission in iOS Settings.
  • For App Store purchases or refunds, use Apple's purchase history and support flow.
