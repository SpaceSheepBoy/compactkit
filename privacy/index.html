PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy,
plain and specific.

Just Compress is designed around local file processing. Web tools run in your browser where possible, and iOS app pages below describe each app's exact data handling.

SummaryJust Compress does not require an account. Browser tools process selected files on your device and do not upload those files to Just Compress. Companion iOS apps are designed for local processing and have app-specific privacy pages.

Effective date: June 4, 2026

Information we do not intentionally collect

  • Your selected documents, photos, videos, or audio files.
  • Your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or advertising identifiers.
  • Account credentials, because Just Compress does not require accounts for the browser tools.

Browser tools

Just Compress web tools use browser APIs and WebAssembly libraries to process files locally when you choose a file. Selected files stay in your browser session and are not uploaded to Just Compress servers.

iOS apps

Each iOS app has its own public privacy policy describing permissions, local data, purchases, diagnostics, and third-party services. Use the app-specific pages below for App Store metadata and in-app legal links.

Diagnostics and analytics

Apple may provide aggregated crash reports, performance information, and App Store analytics to developers. These reports are controlled by Apple and depend on user device settings. Just Compress uses them to improve reliability.

Privacy choices

You can manage app permissions in iOS Settings. You can delete app-local files and preferences by deleting the app from your device. For questions, contact abel0911@icloud.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as Just Compress changes. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
