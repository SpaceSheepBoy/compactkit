How to Make a PDF from Photos on iPhone

Combine several photos, receipts, scans, or screenshots into one PDF.

Watch on YouTube

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open PDF Compressor on your iPhone.
  2. Choose the photos, receipts, scans, or screenshots you want in the document.
  3. Put them in the order you want.
  4. Export one PDF file and save or share it.
