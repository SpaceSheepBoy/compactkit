How to Add a Watermark to a PDF on iPhone

Add a visible purpose mark before sending sensitive documents for renting, visas, forms, banking, or ID copies.

Watch on YouTube

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open PDF Watermarker on your iPhone.
  2. Choose the PDF you want to protect.
  3. Type the text you want to stamp, such as CONFIDENTIAL, DRAFT, or a clear purpose note.
  4. Adjust the preview, then export the watermarked copy.

What app do I need?

Use PDF Watermarker: Stamp Docs. You can open the App Store link, or search PDF Watermarker Stamp Docs in the App Store.
