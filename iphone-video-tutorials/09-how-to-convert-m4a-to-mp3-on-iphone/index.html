How to Convert M4A to MP3 on iPhone

Convert an iPhone voice memo or M4A file into MP3 for easier sharing and compatibility.

Watch on YouTube

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open Audio Compressor on your iPhone.
  2. Choose the M4A file or voice memo.
  3. Select MP3 as the output format.
  4. Export the MP3 file for easier sharing or compatibility.

What app do I need?

Use Audio Compressor: Reduce Size. You can open the App Store link, or search Audio Compressor Reduce Size in the App Store.
