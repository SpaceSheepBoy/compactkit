How to Extract Audio from Video on iPhone

Save the audio track from a video when you only need the sound.

Watch on YouTube

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open Audio Compressor on your iPhone.
  2. Choose the video that contains the sound you need.
  3. Select the option to extract audio from video.
  4. Export the audio file and save or share it.

What app do I need?
