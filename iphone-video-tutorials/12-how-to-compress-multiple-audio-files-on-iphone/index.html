How to Compress Multiple Audio Files on iPhone

Reduce several audio files together when you have many recordings to send or store.

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open Audio Compressor on your iPhone.
  2. Select the audio files you want to reduce together.
  3. Choose the compression setting for the batch.
  4. Export the smaller recordings and save time.
