How to Compress PDF on iPhone

Make one PDF smaller so it is easier to upload, email, or store.

Watch on YouTube

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open PDF Compressor on your iPhone.
  2. Choose the PDF that is too large.
  3. Pick a size setting that fits your need.
  4. Export the smaller PDF and try the upload or email again.

What app do I need?

Use PDF Compressor: Sign & Merge. You can open the App Store link, or search PDF Compressor Sign Merge in the App Store.
