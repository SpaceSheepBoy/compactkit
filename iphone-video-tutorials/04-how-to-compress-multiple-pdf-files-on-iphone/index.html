How to Compress Multiple PDF Files on iPhone

Reduce many PDF files at once before emailing, uploading, or saving storage.

Watch on YouTube

Quick steps

This is for iPhone users who want to finish the job without learning a big app. The video shows the full flow, and these steps keep it simple.

  1. Open PDF Compressor on your iPhone.
  2. Select the PDF files you want to reduce.
  3. Choose a smaller-size setting for the batch.
  4. Export the smaller files and use them for email, upload, or storage.

What app do I need?

Use PDF Compressor: Sign & Merge. You can open the App Store link, or search PDF Compressor Sign Merge in the App Store.
